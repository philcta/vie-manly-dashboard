The Profit Squeeze: What's Happening

1. Labour costs climbing faster than revenue

Labour increased +37% ($32K → $44K) while net sales only grew +7%. This is the #1 driver of the profit squeeze.

2. Average sale declining (−10% from peak)

From $24.64 (Oct) to $22.19 (Feb). More customers through the door, but spending less per visit.

3. Cafe mix increasing (double-edged sword)

Cafe share grew from ~22% to ~29%. Higher margin (70%) but much higher labour intensity — the extra margin is consumed by extra barista labour.

6 Key Actions (Ordered by Impact)

1. 🎯 Control Labour Scheduling — HIGHEST IMPACT

Dashboard tool: Labour % chart → day-of-week filter; Staff page for shift-level detail
  • Identify highest-cost shifts (Sunday/public holiday penalty rates are 2x)
  • Cross-reference daily Labour % with Net Sales: cut hours where Labour % > 30%
  • Audit whether all rostered hours are necessary — especially mid-week slow periods
  • Target: get Labour % back below 24%
Estimated saving: $5K–$8K per month ($60-96K/year)

2. 💰 Lift Average Sale Value

Dashboard tool: Avg Sale chart (downward trend visible); Members page for spending patterns
  • Review Members page for spending pattern changes
  • Implement upsell strategies: combo deals (coffee + bakery item)
  • Check if discount frequency increased (compare Gross vs Net spread)
  • Target: floor at $24.00 avg sale
Revenue uplift: $1 improvement = +$6,800/month pure margin

3. 🏬 Optimize Retail Category Mix

Dashboard tool: Category chart view; Inventory page → sort by Actual Profit column
  • Increase shelf space for categories at 42%+ margin (Tea, Gifts, Fermented Goods)
  • Consider dropping items consistently below 38% margin
  • Monitor Category chart monthly to track high-margin category growth
Potential uplift: 1-2 pts on overall margin = $10-20K/year

4. 👥 Leverage the Members Program

Dashboard tool: Members page → revenue %, status breakdown; Campaigns page for re-engagement
  • Identify "Cooling" (14-30d) and "At Risk" (30-45d) members → SMS campaigns
  • Promote loyalty sign-ups at checkout
  • Target: increase member revenue share by 5%

5. 📦 Fix Dead Stock & Overstock

Dashboard tool: Inventory page → Stock Intelligence alerts
  • Run clearance on items with sell-through < 10% and days-of-stock > 90
  • Tighten reorder quantities for slow-movers
  • Track "Needs Action" KPI — target −30% reduction
Capital release: $5K–$15K back into cash flow

6. 📊 Weekly Performance Reviews

Dashboard tool: "This week" / "Last week" with "vs. Prior period" comparison
  • Every Monday: Check "Last week" vs prior week — focus on Labour % and Avg Sale
  • Flag: any day where Labour % > 30% — discuss with team
  • Monthly: review "This month" vs "Last month" to catch drift early
