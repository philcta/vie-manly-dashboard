## The Profit Squeeze: What's Happening

Three forces are compressing real profitability despite growing top-line revenue:

### 1. Labour costs climbing faster than revenue
- **Sep-Oct**: Labour ~$32K → **21-22%** of sales
- **Jan-Feb**: Labour ~$43-44K → **28%** of sales
- Labour increased **+37%** ($32K → $44K) while net sales only grew **+7%**
- **This is the #1 driver of the squeeze**

### 2. Average sale declining (more transactions, smaller baskets)
- **Oct peak**: $24.64 per transaction
- **Feb trough**: $22.19 per transaction (−10%)
- More customers through the door, but each spending less individually

### 3. Cafe mix increasing (double-edged sword)
- Cafe share grew from ~22% to ~29% of total sales
- Cafe has **higher margin** (70%) but **much higher labour intensity**
- The extra margin is consumed by extra barista/food prep labour

## 6 Key Actions (Ordered by Impact)

### 1. 🎯 Control Labour Scheduling — *HIGHEST IMPACT*

**Dashboard tool**: Labour % chart → filter by day-of-week; Staff page for shift-level granularity

Labour jumped from 22% to 28% in 3 months — that's **$10K+ per month** in wages not covered by proportional revenue growth.

**Actions**:
- Use the **Staff page** to identify highest-cost shifts (Sunday/public holiday penalty rates are 2x)
- Cross-reference daily **Labour %** with daily **Net Sales**: cut staffing on days where Labour % exceeds 30%
- Audit whether all rostered hours are necessary — especially mid-week slow periods
- **Target**: get Labour % back below 24%
- **Estimated saving**: $5K–$8K per month at current revenue levels (~$60-96K/year)

---

### 2. 💰 Lift Average Sale Value

**Dashboard tool**: Avg Sale chart (visible downward trend); Members page for spending patterns

Average sale dropped from $24.64 to $22.19 — that's $2.45 per transaction × 6,800 monthly transactions = **~$16,600/month in lost revenue** at zero extra cost.

**Actions**:
- Review the **Members page** for spending pattern changes: are high-value members declining?
- Implement upsell strategies: **combo deals** (coffee + bakery item, smoothie + protein bar)
- Check if discount frequency has increased (compare Gross vs Net sales spread)
- Monitor daily Avg Sale trend with the chart — set a floor target of $24.00
- **Revenue uplift**: Even a $1 improvement = **+$6,800/month** of mostly pure margin

---

### 3. 🏬 Optimize Retail Category Mix

**Dashboard tool**: Category view on charts; Inventory page → sort by Actual Profit column

Retail margin is 40.9% on average, but it's not uniform across categories.

**High margin** (42-48%): Tea, Gifts, Fermented Goods, Dips, Pasta Sauces
**Low margin** (35-39%): Home Fragrance, Baby, Superfoods, Seafood

**Actions**:
- Use **Inventory** → sort by Actual Profit column to identify margin killers
- Increase shelf space and in-store promotion for categories at 42%+ margin
- Consider dropping or repricing items consistently below 38% margin
- Monitor the **Category chart** monthly to see if high-margin categories are growing their share
- **Potential uplift**: 1-2 percentage points on overall margin = $10-20K/year

---

### 4. 👥 Leverage the Members Program

**Dashboard tool**: Members page → Member Revenue %, spending patterns, status breakdown

The loyalty program is a competitive moat. Members spend more consistently and have higher lifetime value.

**Actions**:
- Monitor **Member Revenue %** trend on the Members page — is member share growing or declining?
- Identify **"Cooling"** (14-30d) and **"At Risk"** (30-45d) members → target with SMS campaigns
- Use the **Campaigns page** to send re-engagement offers to churned high-value members
- Promote loyalty sign-ups at checkout — every member captured increases repeat visit probability
- **Target**: increase member revenue share by 5% = meaningful basket size improvement

---

### 5. 📦 Fix Dead Stock & Overstock

**Dashboard tool**: Inventory page → Stock Intelligence alerts (Critical, Low, Overstock, Dead Stock)

Capital tied up in non-moving inventory is money that could be working elsewhere.

**Actions**:
- Use **Inventory filters** for "Dead Stock" and "Overstock" alerts weekly
- Run clearance on items with sell-through < 10% and days-of-stock > 90
- Tighten reorder quantities for slow-movers to reduce capital lock-up
- Track the **"Needs Action"** KPI card — target reducing it by 30%
- **Capital release**: potentially $5K–$15K back into cash flow

---

### 6. 📊 Weekly Performance Reviews (Discipline)

**Dashboard tool**: "This week" / "Last week" with "vs. Prior period" comparison

Use the dashboard as a **weekly operating discipline**, not just a monthly backward look.

**Weekly cadence**:
- **Every Monday**: Check "Last week" vs prior week — focus on **Labour %** and **Avg Sale**
- **Flag**: any day where Labour % > 30% — discuss with team immediately
- **Track**: the **Real Profit %** chart trend line — if it's flat or rising, you're winning
- **Monthly**: review "This month" vs "Last month" to catch drift before it becomes a problem
