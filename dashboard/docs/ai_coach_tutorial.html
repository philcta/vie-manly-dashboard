Pro Tips

Get the most out of your AI Coach

📅

Monday Ritual: Start every Monday by asking "Give me my Monday morning briefing". This gives you a complete review of last week + focused priorities for this week. Takes 30 seconds.

🎯

Daily Wins: Each morning, ask "Give me a daily sales goal: which 3 products should I actively promote today?". Share this with your team — it creates a mini-mission for the day.

🔄

Follow-Up Questions: The coach remembers your conversation. After getting a recommendation, ask "How exactly would I implement that?" or "Give me the step-by-step."

📊

Weekly Score: Every Friday, ask "Score my week out of 10". Track this number week over week — it's your business fitness score. Aim for consistent 7+ across all categories.
