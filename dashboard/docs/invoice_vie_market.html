Deliverables Specification
INV-002 — VIE Market — Scope of work delivered February – March 2026
Deliverable 1 — Live Dashboard Application

Cloud-hosted (Vercel), mobile-responsive real-time dashboard connected to Square POS via Supabase with automated hourly data sync. Accessible 24/7 from any device.

📊 Overview — KPI Cards & Charts
12 animated KPI cards with period comparison
  • Net Sales, Gross Sales, Cafe/Retail Net Sales split
  • Transactions (Cafe/Retail), Customers, Average Sale (Cafe/Retail)
  • Labour Cost & Labour vs Sales % with Cafe/Retail split
  • Avg Profit Margin — weighted by sales mix (Cafe/Retail)
  • Real Profit Margin — after COGS + labour (Cafe/Retail)
  • Real Profit $ — after COGS + labour
  • Interactive multi-metric trend chart with moving average
  • Flexible period selector: today, week, month, quarter, FY, custom
👥 Members & Loyalty
Full member intelligence with loyalty program analytics
  • Member KPIs: active members, revenue %, avg spend, visit frequency
  • Status classification: Active, Cooling, At Risk, Churned
  • Per-member: all-time vs 30-day spending, Cafe/Retail split, trend %
  • Loyalty points tracking: lifetime, redeemed, available balance
  • Sortable, searchable member table with CSV export
  • Historical member revenue chart with comparison periods
📦 Inventory & Stock Intelligence
Real-time stock monitoring with predictive reorder alerts
  • Stock Value (GST inc./ex.), Retail Value, Avg Margin
  • Alerts: Critical, Low, Watch, Overstock, Dead Stock
  • Per-item: velocity, sold 7/30/90d, days of stock, sell-through %
  • Multi-filter by category, vendor, alert level
  • Sortable/searchable table with CSV export
👷 Staff & Labour
Labour cost computation with award-rate compliance
  • Per-staff earnings: weekday, Saturday, Sunday, public holiday rates
  • Configurable hourly rates editor with historical support
  • 80/20 Barista split (Cafe/Retail labour allocation)
  • Daily labour cost chart and shift-level breakdown
💬 SMS Campaigns
Campaign-ready infrastructure for member re-engagement
  • Member segmentation by status, spend level, and activity
  • Campaign creation interface with target audience filters
⚙️ Settings & Configuration
System configuration and category management
  • Cafe vs Retail category classification
  • Staff rate configuration
  • System and integration settings
Deliverable 2 — Business Improvement Plan

Comprehensive data-driven analysis with 6 prioritised actions, delivered as PDF and Markdown.

  • Monthly performance analysis — Net sales, transactions, avg sale, labour cost, labour %, and Cafe/Retail mix trends (Aug 2025 – Mar 2026)
  • Profit squeeze diagnosis — Root cause analysis of labour cost growth outpacing revenue
  • 6 prioritised actions — Labour scheduling control ($60-96K/yr impact), average sale uplift, retail category mix optimisation, member program leverage, dead stock clearance, weekly performance review cadence
  • KPI targets — Measurable targets with timelines (Labour ≤24%, Avg Sale ≥$24, Real Profit Margin ≥25%)
