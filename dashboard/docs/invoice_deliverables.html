Real-Time Business Intelligence Dashboard

Custom-built web application for VIE. MANLY — Delivered March 2026

Deliverable 1 — Live Dashboard Application

Cloud-hosted (Vercel), mobile-responsive web dashboard connected to Square POS via Supabase, with automated hourly data sync. Accessible 24/7 from any device.

Overview Page — KPI Cards & Charts
12 animated KPI cards with period comparison (prior period, same period last year). Flexible period selector: today, this week/month, last week/month, quarter, financial year (AU), and custom date range.
  • Net Sales, Gross Sales, Cafe/Retail Net Sales split
  • Transactions (Cafe/Retail), Customers, Average Sale (Cafe/Retail)
  • Labour Cost (Cafe/Retail $), Labour vs Sales % (Cafe/Retail)
  • Avg Profit Margin (weighted by sales mix, Cafe/Retail)
  • Real Profit Margin (after COGS + labour, Cafe/Retail)
  • Real Profit $ (after COGS + labour)
  • Interactive multi-metric chart: Net Sales, Transactions, Customers, Avg Sale, Real Profit %, Labour % — with 3-month moving average and trend line
Members & Loyalty Page
Full member intelligence with loyalty program analytics.
  • Member KPIs: active members, revenue %, avg spend, visit frequency
  • Member status classification: Active, Cooling, At Risk, Churned
  • Per-member details: all-time vs 30-day spending, Cafe/Retail split, trend %
  • Loyalty points: lifetime, redeemed, available balance
  • Sortable, searchable member table with CSV export
  • Historical member revenue chart with comparison periods
Inventory & Stock Intelligence Page
Real-time stock monitoring with predictive reorder alerts.
  • Stock Value (GST inc./ex.), Retail Value, Avg Profit Margin
  • Stock Intelligence panel: Critical, Low, Watch, Overstock, Dead Stock alerts
  • Per-item: on-hand qty, sales velocity, sold 7/30/90d, days of stock, sell-through %
  • Multi-filter: by category, vendor, alert level
  • Full sortable/searchable table with CSV export
Staff & Labour Page
Labour cost computation engine with award-rate compliance.
  • Per-staff earnings: weekday, Saturday, Sunday, public holiday rates
  • Configurable hourly rates editor with historical rate support
  • 80/20 Barista split (Cafe/Retail allocation)
  • Daily labour cost chart and shift-level breakdown
SMS Campaigns Page
Campaign-ready infrastructure for member re-engagement.
  • Member segmentation by status, spend level, and activity
  • Campaign creation interface with target audience filters
Settings & Configuration Page
Category classification management (Cafe vs Retail mapping), staff rate configuration, and system settings.

Deliverable 2 — Business Improvement Plan (Report)

Comprehensive data-driven analysis with 6 prioritised actions, delivered as PDF and Markdown.

  • Monthly performance analysis — Net sales, transactions, avg sale, labour cost, labour %, and Cafe/Retail mix trends (Aug 2025 – Mar 2026)
  • Profit squeeze diagnosis — Root cause analysis of labour cost growth outpacing revenue
  • 6 prioritised actions — Labour scheduling control ($60-96K/yr impact), average sale uplift, retail category mix optimisation, member program leverage, dead stock clearance, weekly performance review cadence
  • KPI targets — Measurable targets with timelines (Labour ≤24%, Avg Sale ≥$24, Real Profit Margin ≥25%)
Stack: Next.js · TypeScript · Supabase (PostgreSQL) · Vercel · Square API integration · Recharts · Framer Motion
